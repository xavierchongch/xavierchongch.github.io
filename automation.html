T2 Extract

→

Bulk T2 data extraction into OneSource Tax Provision — RTP in hours, not days.

PythonOneSourceT2GIFISch 1Sch 4Sch 8EIFELRTP

Python tool that reads T2 corporate returns across 70+ entities in bulk and feeds the data directly into OneSource Tax Provision workpapers. RTP that used to take days gets done accurately in hours.

  • Sch 1Each line maps to an OTP adjustment code — RTP is automated, not manual. Inconsistent descriptions across entities get flagged so nothing slips through.
  • Sch 4Loss continuity pulled from the return is compared against the provision balance to identify where an RTP entry is needed.
  • Sch 8 & 13CCA claims and reserve balances are validated against supporting workpapers and traced through to Schedule 1 to confirm the flow is clean.
  • Sch 130EIFEL data is extracted and reviewed to confirm the best interest deductibility position is captured and the return is free of errors under ITA s.18.2 / s.18.21.
  • Tax dueExtracts tax payable and payment history across all entities — used to calculate instalments, run cash tax analyses, determine final payment amounts and reconcile against the tax payable rollforward on the provision side.
02 / 03

Reg 105 Automation

→

End-to-end Reg 105 withholding compliance — SAP pull to CRA reconciliation in one run.

PythonSAP GUI ScriptingPower QueryReg 105Part XIIIReconciliation

End-to-end Regulation 105 withholding tax compliance tool built in Python with SAP GUI Scripting and Power Query. Replaces a largely manual multi-step process with a single run that handles everything from data pull to reconciliation.

  • RolloverPrior period workbook is automatically rolled forward — structure, formulas and reference data carry over with no manual setup.
  • SAP pullGL detail is extracted directly from SAP via GUI Scripting. Invoices are downloaded in the same run — no switching between screens or manual exports.
  • WHT checkInvoice line items are read and the system-calculated withholding is compared against the actual amount withheld. Variances are flagged for review.
  • Vendor recVendor-level reconciliation is produced automatically — ties withholding amounts back to each vendor across the period.
  • CRA recCRA reconciliation is generated to confirm total remittances agree with what was reported, closing the loop before filing.

Instalment Calculator

→

70+ entity instalment workbook — minimum method, cash flow optimized, all outputs in one place.

ExcelITA s.157InstalmentsCash taxProvincial allocation

Excel workbook covering 70+ entities across multiple brands. Fed by tax return data, prior and current period provision and provincial allocation — it calculates instalments in both USD and CAD and picks the minimum method under ITA s.157(1) to optimize cash flow.

  • MethodCompares three instalment bases (prior-prior year, prior year and current year estimate) and automatically selects the minimum for each entity to optimize cash outflow. Hybrid 2+10 method is also supported.
  • ExemptionEntities below the $3,000 CAD threshold are automatically flagged as exempt. Entities are grouped into "Require Instalment" and "Exempt" for cleaner review.
  • PaymentsYTD payments and prior year overpayments are tracked per entity. Completed months use actual paid amounts; future months are formula-driven.
  • DeferralDeferral summary shows how much tax is deferred to the next year, broken out by entity and by brand with a percentage deferred column for management review.
  • OutputsBrand-level instalment summary (USD and CAD), monthly dashboard, payment request with federal/Quebec/Alberta provincial splits and a deferral analysis — all in one workbook.
