The Provision Cycle
01

Current Tax

Current tax expense each period across all jurisdictions.

Current taxOneSource Tax ProvisionASC 740
  • ComputeCalculate current tax expense for each entity based on taxable income and applicable federal and provincial/state rates.
  • AllocateApply provincial allocation percentages (Canada) and state apportionment factors (US) to compute jurisdiction-level current tax.
  • True-upAdjust the current tax balance for prior-year true-ups identified during the RTP process.
02

Tax Payable

Rollforward, balance proof and GL reconciliation.

Tax payableRollforwardBalance proofGL reconciliation
  • RollforwardMaintain the tax payable rollforward each period — opening balance, current tax expense, payments, refunds and adjustments.
  • Balance proofPerform an ending tax payable balance proof — compare the computed payable to the GL balance and determine whether a payable true-up entry is needed.
  • ReconcileTie the tax payable balance to the balance sheet and resolve any unexplained variances before period close.
03

Deferred Tax

Full deferred tax roll — temporary differences, rate changes and balance proof.

Deferred taxTemporary differencesRate reconciliationASC 740
  • RollMaintain the deferred tax asset and liability roll — opening balance, current period movements and rate change adjustments across federal and provincial/state jurisdictions.
  • Balance proofPerform an ending deferred tax balance proof each period — compare the computed DTA/DTL to the GL balance and determine whether a deferred true-up entry is needed.
  • True-upPerform the deferred tax true-up at year end — compare provision deferred tax to the as-filed return positions and book the adjustment.
  • PresentationPrepare net deferred tax balances for financial statement presentation per jurisdiction.
04

Return-to-Provision

Systematic RTP across 70+ entities — every adjustment traced, coded and posted.

RTPT2 ExtractOneSource Tax ProvisionAdjustment codes
  • ExtractPull Schedule 1 data from T2 returns across all entities using T2 Extract — each line mapped to an OTP adjustment code automatically.
  • AnalyzeCompare tax return positions to provision estimates — identify permanent and temporary differences requiring true-up entries.
  • PostBook RTP journal entries and update the deferred tax roll to reflect the as-filed positions.
05

FIN 48 — Uncertain Tax Positions

Recognition and measurement of UTPs each period.

FIN 48ASC 740-10UTPsMLTN threshold
  • RecognizeApply the more-likely-than-not threshold to each identified position — document the technical basis for recognition or non-recognition.
  • MeasureCompute the cumulative probability distribution and determine the measured benefit for each recognized position.
  • RollforwardMaintain the UTP rollforward each period — additions, reductions, settlements and lapse of statute of limitations.
  • DisclosePrepare FIN 48 disclosures for the 10-K and 10-Q tax footnote including a reconciliation of the beginning and ending unrecognized tax benefit balance.
06

FIN 18 — Interim Reporting

Quarterly provision using the annual effective tax rate method.

FIN 18AETRInterim provisionASC 740-270
  • AETREstimate the annual effective tax rate each quarter based on projected full-year ordinary income and tax — updated as the year progresses.
  • ApplyApply the AETR to year-to-date ordinary income to compute the interim tax provision. Discrete items are recognized in the period they occur.
  • ReconcileReconcile the interim provision to the prior quarter and explain material movements for management reporting.
07

Valuation Allowance

Quarterly VA assessment — weighing positive and negative evidence.

Valuation allowanceDTA realizabilityASC 740-10-30
  • AssessEvaluate each jurisdiction's deferred tax asset position against the four sources of taxable income under ASC 740-10-30.
  • EvidenceWeigh positive evidence (future taxable income, reversing temporary differences, tax planning strategies) against negative evidence (cumulative losses, expiring attributes).
  • DocumentPrepare the VA memo supporting the conclusion — used by external auditors and referenced in the tax footnote disclosure.
08

ETR Analysis

Rate reconciliation that explains why the effective tax rate moved.

ETRRate reconciliationStatutory ratePermanent items
  • BridgeBuild the rate reconciliation from statutory rate to effective rate — permanent items, rate differentials, uncertain positions, VA movements and discrete items.
  • NarrativeWrite the ETR commentary explaining quarter-over-quarter and year-over-year drivers for management and the audit committee.
  • ForecastPrepare the projected full-year ETR for FIN 18 and update it each quarter as new information arises.
09

Tax Journal Entries

Book all tax entries each period.

Journal entriesCurrent expenseDeferred expenseUTPDTA/DTL

Key entries each period:

  • CurrentCurrent tax expense and tax payable.
  • DeferredDeferred tax expense, DTA and DTL movements.
  • RTPReturn-to-provision expense and related balance sheet adjustments.
  • UTPUncertain tax position expense and UTP liability balance.
  • VAValuation allowance adjustments against DTA.
  • True-upPayable and deferred true-up entries when balance proofs identify a variance.
