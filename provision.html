Tax Provision

Full-cycle ASC 740 — from quarterly provision to year-end close.

Tax provision support across a global entity portfolio, with a focus on accurate reporting, clear reconciliations and workpapers that can stand up to management and auditor review.

The Provision Cycle
01

Current Tax

Current tax expense supported by consistent source data and reviewable calculations.

Current taxOneSource Tax ProvisionASC 740
  • CalculatePrepare current tax expense support using taxable income, jurisdictional rates and entity-level filing positions.
  • AllocateApply provincial allocation percentages and state apportionment factors consistently across entities.
  • ReviewIdentify prior-year true-ups through the RTP process and support the related current tax adjustments.
02

Tax Payable

Tax payable rollforward, balance proof and GL reconciliation.

Tax payableRollforwardBalance proofGL reconciliation
  • RollforwardMaintain the tax payable rollforward each period, including current tax expense, payments, refunds and adjustments.
  • ProofCompare the computed payable to the GL balance and identify whether a true-up entry is needed.
  • ResolveInvestigate unexplained variances before close so the balance sheet support is complete and review-ready.
03

Deferred Tax

Deferred tax rollforward, temporary differences and balance proof.

Deferred taxTemporary differencesRate reconciliationASC 740
  • RollMaintain deferred tax asset and liability support for temporary differences, rate changes and current period movements.
  • ProofCompare computed DTA/DTL balances to the GL and identify any deferred true-up entries needed for close.
  • True-upCompare provision deferred tax to as-filed return positions and support the year-end adjustment.
  • PresentPrepare net deferred tax balances for financial statement presentation by jurisdiction.
04

Return-to-Provision

RTP review across a multi-entity portfolio — adjustments traced, explained and recorded.

RTPT2 ExtractOneSource Tax ProvisionAdjustment codes
  • ReviewReview Schedule 1 data from T2 returns across the entity portfolio and map return adjustments to provision categories.
  • AnalyzeCompare tax return positions to provision estimates and identify permanent or temporary differences requiring true-up entries.
  • RecordSupport RTP journal entries and update the deferred tax roll to reflect as-filed positions.
05

ASC 740 — Uncertain Tax Positions (FIN 48)

Recognition, measurement and disclosure support for uncertain tax positions.

ASC 740-10-25ASC 740-10-30FIN 48UTPs
  • AssessReview identified positions and document the technical basis for recognition or non-recognition.
  • MeasureSupport measurement of recognized positions and maintain clear support for the benefit recorded.
  • RollforwardMaintain the UTP rollforward, including additions, reductions, settlements and statute changes.
  • DisclosePrepare uncertain tax position disclosure support for quarterly and annual tax footnotes.
06

ASC 740-270 — Interim Reporting (FIN 18)

Quarterly provision using the annual effective tax rate method.

ASC 740-270FIN 18AETRInterim provisionDiscrete items
  • EstimateSupport the annual effective tax rate each quarter using projected ordinary income and current tax assumptions.
  • ApplyApply the AETR to year-to-date ordinary income and separately identify discrete items in the period they occur.
  • ExplainReconcile the interim provision to the prior quarter and explain material movements for management reporting.
07

Valuation Allowance

Quarterly VA assessment — weighing positive and negative evidence.

Valuation allowanceDTA realizabilityASC 740-10-30
  • AssessEvaluate deferred tax asset realizability by jurisdiction under ASC 740-10-30.
  • EvidenceWeigh positive and negative evidence, including future taxable income, reversing temporary differences and cumulative loss history.
  • DocumentPrepare valuation allowance support used for auditor review and tax footnote disclosure.
08

ETR Analysis

Rate reconciliation that explains why the effective tax rate moved.

ETRRate reconciliationStatutory ratePermanent items
  • BridgePrepare the rate reconciliation from statutory rate to effective rate, including permanent items, rate differentials, uncertain positions, VA movements and discrete items.
  • NarrativeExplain quarter-over-quarter and year-over-year ETR drivers for management reporting.
  • UpdateRefresh the projected full-year ETR under ASC 740-270 as new information becomes available.
09

Journal entriesCurrent expenseDeferred expenseUTPDTA/DTL
